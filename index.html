Research

Posted on 2023-11-02

Interested in design of nanophotonic functional devices based on various platforms, including integrated photonic chips, topological photonic crystals and metamaterials.

Algorithm-assisted design methods of nanostructures, including topology optimization, inverse design method and neural networks.

Application of nanophotonic devices in different fields like information transmission, mathematical operation, optical computation and so on.
